Suggested Rules on Service Mark Usage:

  •     Refrain from using the mark as a noun. Instead use the mark as an adjective.
  •     When possible, distinguish the mark in a different font or color from the rest of the text.
  •     Mark the service mark with the "sm" symbol to designate the mark as your common law service mark.
